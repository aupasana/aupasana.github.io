--- layout: post title: ṛṣyādi nyāsaḥ date: '2015-02-21T10:47:00.002-08:00' author: Unknown tags: - nyāsa - sanskrit modified_time: '2015-02-21T10:47:54.985-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1404870071299307321.post-8533801173699912520 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.aupasana.com/2015/02/rsyadi-nyasah.html --- The locations for the ṛṣyādi nyāsa are given in the following verse in the prapañca sāra sāra saṁgraha --

ṛṣir gurutvāt śirasaiva dhāryaḥ chando'kṣaratvāt rasanāgataṁ syāt |
dhiyā'vagantavyatayā sadaiva hṛdi pradiṣṭā manudevatā ca ||

i.e. the ṛṣi is placed on the head, the chandas on the tongue, and the devatā is established in the heart.
